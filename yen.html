研究經驗 Research Experience

壓電材料研發專案 Piezoelectric Material Development Project

大學三年級時參與壓電材料研究，成功開發出壓電複合材料感測器並獲得專利授權；隨後進行鐵電電容器整合場效電晶體與柔性複合材料奈米壓電發電機的研究，獲得國際競賽肯定。 In my junior year, I participated in piezoelectric material research and successfully developed a piezoelectric composite sensor that received a patent. Later, I worked on integrating ferroelectric capacitors with field-effect transistors and flexible composite nano piezoelectric generators, earning international recognition.

專利成就 Patents

壓電複合材料感測器 Piezoelectric Composite Sensor

專利號碼：Patent No.: M655946

授權日期：Date Granted: 2024/05/21

開發的新型壓電複合材料感測器具備高靈敏度與穩定性，適用於航空與醫療領域。 The newly developed piezoelectric composite sensor offers high sensitivity and stability, making it suitable for aerospace and medical applications.

鐵電電容器整合場效電晶體 Ferroelectric Capacitor Integrated with FET

專利號碼：Patent No.: M565262

授權日期：Date Granted: 2024/06/01

整合技術大幅提升了電子元件的性能與能效。 This integration technology significantly enhances the performance and energy efficiency of electronic components.

柔性複合材料奈米壓電發電機結構改良 Improved Structure for Flexible Composite Nano Piezoelectric Generator

專利號碼：Patent No.: M657451

授權日期：Date Granted: 2024/07/01

結構改良後的奈米壓電發電機不僅具備良好的柔性與耐用性，更適用於可穿戴設備與能源收集。 With an improved structure, the nano piezoelectric generator not only offers excellent flexibility and durability but is also ideal for wearable devices and energy harvesting.
